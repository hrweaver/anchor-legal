# Anchor Support

Need help with Anchor? We're a small team and we read everything.

## Contact us

**Email: [support@anchorph.one](mailto:support@anchorph.one)**

We usually reply within 1 business day.

## Reporting a problem

The fastest way to get a bug fixed is from inside the app:

1. Open Anchor and go to the settings screen
2. Scroll to the **Feedback** section
3. Tap **Report a Bug** and describe what happened
4. You'll get an 8-character reference ID. Include it if you email us; it lets us see the
   technical details alongside your report.

Feature ideas are welcome too, via **Request a Feature** in the same section.

## Common questions

**How do I pair with my partner?**
Both of you install Anchor. One of you opens the pairing screen and shows their code (or QR);
the other enters or scans it. Pairing always requires both people to opt in.

**How do I unpair?**
Settings → Manage Partners → Remove. Your partner is notified. Either person can unpair at
any time.

**An app I need is blocked. How do I open it?**
Use **Request Access** in the app. Your partner gets a notification and can approve it for a
time you both agree on. In Solo mode, your self-set PIN unlocks it.

**How do I delete my account and data?**
Settings → Advanced → Delete My Data / Delete Account. Details in our
[Account Deletion policy](ACCOUNT_DELETION.md).
