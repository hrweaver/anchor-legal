# Deleting Your Anchor Account

**App:** Anchor – Digital Wellbeing
**Developer:** Harry Weaver

Anchor lets you delete your account and all associated data at any time. There
are two ways to do it.

## Option 1 — Delete from inside the app (recommended)

1. Open **Anchor**.
2. Tap the Anchor app on your home screen to open **Settings**.
3. Scroll to **Advanced**.
4. Tap **Delete My Account** (shown as "Delete My Data" on older versions).
5. Confirm. If you have an account with a password, you'll be asked to re-enter
   it to confirm the deletion.

This immediately removes your account and your data.

## Option 2 — Request deletion by email

If you can't access the app, email **harry.r.weaver@gmail.com** from the address
associated with your account, with the subject **"Delete my Anchor account"**.
We will delete your account and data within 30 days and confirm by reply.

## What gets deleted

When you delete your account, we permanently remove:

- Your account and sign-in (email/password)
- Your name and pairing code
- Your accountability-partner connection(s)
- App usage history, blocked-attempt records, and daily summaries
- Allowed-apps lists, app time limits, and schedules
- Alerts and removal suggestions

## What is kept (and for how long)

- **Anonymous, non-identifying records** used for aggregate service metrics
  (e.g. anonymous daily-active-user pings that are not linked to your identity)
  may be retained.
- **Bug reports** you submitted are retained for up to **90 days**, then deleted.
- Backups and logs containing personally identifiable data are purged within
  **30 days** of deletion.

For full details on what we collect and how long we keep it, see our
[Privacy Policy](https://github.com/hrweaver/anchor-legal/blob/main/PRIVACY.md).
